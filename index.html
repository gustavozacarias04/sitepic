About DEMS

DEMS is an advanced real-time monitoring solution that improves emergency response and healthcare management. Using high-precision sensors and artificial intelligence, it continuously tracks vital signs, allowing healthcare professionals to make quick, informed decisions. With predictive algorithms and real-time data analysis, it helps identify health risks before they become critical, enhancing patient outcomes and transforming emergency care.
